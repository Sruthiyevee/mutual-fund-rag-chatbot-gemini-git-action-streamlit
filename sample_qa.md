# Sample Q&A for Mutual Fund Text RAG Chatbot

Here are sample queries with the assistant’s answers and source links, demonstrating the chatbot's capabilities.

## 1. General Mutual Fund Knowledge
**User:** What are the advantages of investing in mutual funds?

**Assistant:**
Investing in mutual funds offers several meaningful benefits:
*   **Professional Management:** Experienced fund managers handle the investments and make decisions based on market research.
*   **Diversification:** Mutual funds invest in a wide range of securities, which helps spread risk across different sectors and industries.
*   **Liquidity:** Open-ended mutual funds allow you to redeem your units at any time, providing easy access to your money.
*   **Affordability:** You can start investing with small amounts, often as low as ₹500 via SIPs.
*   **Transparency:** Funds regularly disclose their portfolio holdings and performance.
*   **Regulation:** Mutual funds are regulated by SEBI, ensuring investor protection.

**Sources:**

## 3. Service & Operations
**User:** How can I download my Capital Gains Statement?

**Assistant:**
To download your Capital Gains Statement from HDFC Mutual Fund, you can follow these steps:
1.  Visit the **HDFC Mutual Fund website** (hdfcfund.com).
2.  Go to the **'Investor Desk'** or **'Service Request'** section.
3.  Select **'Capital Gains Statement'** from the menu.
4.  Enter your **Folio Number** and **PAN**.
5.  Choose the **Financial Year** for which you need the statement.
6.  You can choose to have it emailed to your registered email ID or download it directly if logged in.

Alternatively, you can also request it via their mobile app or by calling their customer care.

**Sources:**
- `https://www.hdfcfund.com/investor-desk/faqs`

## 7. Educational Content
**User:** What are the different categories of mutual fund schemes?

**Assistant:**
SEBI has categorized mutual fund schemes into five broad groups to ensure uniformity:
1.  **Equity Schemes:** Invest primarily in shares/stocks.
2.  **Debt Schemes:** Invest in fixed income securities like bonds and treasury bills.
3.  **Hybrid Schemes:** Invest in a mix of equity and debt.
4.  **Solution Oriented Schemes:** Designed for specific goals like retirement or children's education.
5.  **Other Schemes:** Includes Index Funds, ETFs, and Fund of Funds.

**Sources:**
- `https://www.amfiindia.com/investor/knowledge-center-info?zoneName=CategorizationOfMutualFundSchemes`
